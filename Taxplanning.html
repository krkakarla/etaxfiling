Tax Planning will help us to reduce the Tax burden and below are list of tax exemption and deduction which we can claim if you have proper documents

Before we go for Tax Planning we better to follow the few guidelines like.

  1. Avoid Bank FD's
      2. For FD amount we can claim deduction under 80C Cumulative Ceiling (1,50,000 rs) however Interest Income is Taxable. Max exemption allowed for interested income is 10,000 rupees so If any income received more than 10,000 rupees is totally taxable. Ex: If you received any interested amount 1,00,000 and your are in with 30% slab then 100000 * 30% is table 30000 is tax amount.

  2. Income From Supermarket
      3. Any Income from Supermarket or Mutual Funds like Short Term or Long Term Capital Gain are totally Taxable

  3. Avoid PF withdrawal before Maturity
      4. Withdrawal of PF amount before maturity is taxble income

  4. Invest in NPS Account
      5. If you have other deductions to claim 80C cumulative ceiling 1,50,000 rs then you are advised to opt for NPS employer Contribution and NPS employee Contribution to get more benefits. Please refer the below table to get the idea how new change will help to get more benefit.
