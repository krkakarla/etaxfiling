Before we go for Tax Planning we better to follow the few guidelines like.

  1. Avoid Bank FD's
      2. For FD amount we can cliam deduction under 80C Cumulative Ceiling (1,50,000 rs) however Interest Income is Taxable. Max exemption allowed for interested income is 10,000 rupess so If any income received more than 10,000 rupees is totally taxable. Ex: If you received any interested amount 1,00,000 and your are in with 30% slab then 100000 * 30% is table 30000 is tax amount.

  2. Income From Sharemarket
      3. Any Income from Sharemarket or Mutual Funds like Short Term or Long Term Capital Gain are totally Taxable

  3. Avoid PF withdrawal before Maturity
      4. Withdrawal of PF amount before maturity is taxble income
